I'm a geospatial information scientist interested in data mining, machine learning, crowdsourcing, and spatial analysis. Currently I work as a data analyst for the Human Factors in GIScience Lab, directed by Dr. Alexander Klippel at Penn State.

I have a master's degree in geographic information science from the Department of Geography at Penn State University. My research looked at crowdsourcing techniques and machine learning techniques to improve land cover datasets.
